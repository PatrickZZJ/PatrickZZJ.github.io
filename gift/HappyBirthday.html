*标题：My Dreams

In my dreams //在我梦里
you are one mighty thunder //你是一道惊雷
blasting through the bottom of the starry sky //打破星空谷底
trembles my heart //震撼我心
dazzles my eyes //让我眼花缭乱

In my dreams //在我梦里
you are the first mizzle of the summer //你是初夏第一缕细雨
waking up the restlessness in the soil //唤醒了泥土里的焦躁不安
increasing the wild nature between the branches and leaves
//增长了枝叶间的蓬勃野性

In my dreams //在我梦里
you are wind in the prairie //你是草原的清风
smell of the ocean //大海的味道
evening sun on the roofline //屋脊上的夕阳
warmth in the quilt //被窝里的温暖

You are many things
in my dreams
//你是我梦中的许多
because you are becoming
everything
to me
//因为你正成为我生命中的所有
小宝贝，这是我们相爱在一起的时光。
生日快乐，一直快快乐乐。
- 爱你的大笨蛋
